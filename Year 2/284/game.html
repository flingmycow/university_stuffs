HOW TO PLAY

Simply place as many pieces of treasure (value from 1-9) or obstacles onto the grid. When you're finished place your robot onto the grid and press END SETUP. If you have correctly followed this then the computer's robot will be placed on the grid then PLAY mode will be initiated!

GOAL OF THE GAME

Using the 'Moves' field, enter movements for your robot (a = left, w = up, x = down, and d = right) and whoever get's the most treasure wins! When there is no treasure left or both robots cannot move then the game finishes. It also ends whenever you decide to end the game. Good luck!
